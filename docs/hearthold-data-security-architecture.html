Design rule: probabilistic components propose; deterministic components dispose. No LLM is ever the security boundary. Policy is signed by the human, enforced at egress, and survives a compromised enforcer (governor pinning fails closed).

human authority deterministic (pure code, verifiable) probabilistic (LLM — propose-only) sealed data at rest enforcement gate (every boundary crossing)

CONTROL PLANE

the human principal · occasional
HUMAN

Principal (Sovereign)

The delegating human. Root of every authority chain. Own DID, own wallet, own keys.

HUMAN

Signet (approver device)

Signs policy (Rulesets) and co-signs sensitive disclosures with fresh proof-of-human. Consent text is never authored by the requesting agent — the policy engine writes what the human reads.

DET

Ruleset chains  = π

Versioned, append-only, per-actor policy documents signed by the Principal. Readers pin the governor's key: a self-signed downgrade by a rooted enforcer is rejected, fails closed. Unsigned policy governs nothing.

PROOF-OF-HUMAN GATEchallenge-bound · single-use txn · decline ⇒ nothing happens

HOME PLANE — TRUSTED HARDWARE

always-on · custodies data · enforces policy · never acts in the world
DET · δ_D

Warden — deterministic policy engine

decideRelease(sensitivity × authorization tier × disclosure mode) at every crossing · per-actor Ruleset checks (kinds / verbs / ceiling / assurance) at egress · authorization ladder: STANDING→LOW · CHALLENGE→MEDIUM · HUMAN→HIGH · MULTIFACTOR→SEALED · deny-by-default.

SEALED

Vault (the 7th Capital)

Personal history, encrypted at rest. Index = embeddings + metadata only, no plaintext. Content unsealed transiently, per cleared request.

AUDIT

Evidence, not logs

Disclosures recorded as salted-Merkle commitments + signed approval statements — transactions, never payloads. Queries not logged.

PROPOSE-ONLY LANE — models are local, labeled machine-derived, and cannot act
PROB · δ_N

Classifier

Local LLM assigns sensitivity. Fail-safe: unclassified = SEALED (most restricted). Human confirms at triage. A jailbroken classifier can only misclassify into quarantine.

PROB · δ_N

Recall (RAG)

Answers the principal's questions from the vault, on-device, ceiling-scoped. Output is fallible, cited, never a verifiable claim by itself.

PROB · δ_N

Agent scripts

Optionally AI-drafted, human-audited before capability grant. Each runs under its own signed Ruleset; the Warden checks its egress. Prompt injection can corrupt an interpretation — it cannot widen an authorization.

EGRESS GATE — decideRelease() + actor Ruleset + ladderwhat leaves: a derived, scoped, expiring, single-use, audience-bound credential — never a raw dump, never a score

WORLD PLANE

phones · browsers · other parties' agents
DET

Emissaries (the principal's envoys)

Carry sealed envelopes and present credentials. Hold no secrets, are never the authority, and are revocable. Kind-scoped delegation credentials bound their reach.

EXTERN

Third-party AI agents / verifiers

Receive purpose-bound grants issued to pairwise DIDs (fresh per relationship — ToIP DTG v0.3). Verify offline against issuer signatures. Reuse refused; expiry enforced; revocation one message away.

EXTERN

Brokers / intermediaries

Relay consent-gated request tickets (no subject identifier, expiring, single-use). Structurally cannot see content — no GDPR custody of data they never process.

TRUST REGISTRY (ToIP TRQP)outward: who may issue · inward: what each agent may do

THREE FLOWS, END TO END

Capture: Emissary witnesses context → sealed envelope home → delegation check → classifier proposes sensitivity (fail-safe SEALED) → human confirms at triage → stored sealed.
Ask (private RAG): principal asks their own vault → recall answers with citations, on-device, ceiling-scoped → nothing leaves the house; query not logged.
Disclose (agentic request): third-party AI sends purpose-bearing, subject-less ticket → Warden authors the consent sentence → Signet: proof-of-human approve/decline → egress gate → derived credential to a fresh pairwise DID → verified offline → burns on reuse, expires on schedule.
